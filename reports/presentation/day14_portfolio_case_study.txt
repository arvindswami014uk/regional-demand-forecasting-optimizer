DAY 14 PORTFOLIO CASE STUDY
================================

Project:
Regional Demand Forecasting and Inventory Placement Optimizer

Context:
I built this capstone to simulate an Amazon-style network planning problem: forecasting regional demand accurately and converting that forecast into inventory placement decisions across a fulfillment network.

Problem:
If regional demand is misread, operations teams face stockouts, costly transfers, excess inventory, and weaker delivery performance.

Approach:
- Benchmarked baseline moving average against Prophet and XGBoost
- Selected XGBoost as best model at 11.06% WMAPE
- Translated forecast outputs into safety stock and reorder point decisions
- Compared speed, cost, and carbon-proxy scenarios
- Finalized a constrained strategy recommendation instead of stopping at unconstrained scenario ranking

Results:
- XGBoost improved forecast accuracy by 42.5% vs baseline
- Safety stock value quantified at \$157,292.60
- Replenishment need estimated at \$780,850.78
- 41 SKU-region combinations flagged as high urgency
- Balanced emerged as the final constrained recommendation

Why this is strong portfolio work:
It demonstrates that I can connect machine learning, inventory policy, and executive decision support in one business storyline instead of treating modeling as an isolated exercise.
