Day 11.5 Narrative Patch — Strategy Differentiation Refinement

The initial Day 11 strategy outputs were too similar across Speed-first, Cost-first, Carbon-first, and Balanced policies.
That indicated the synthetic network assumptions were not creating enough tension between service, cost, and carbon objectives.

To correct this, the Day 11.5 refinement introduced stronger warehouse-level differentiation using:
- warehouse cost multipliers
- warehouse speed multipliers
- warehouse carbon multipliers
- tighter effective capacity pressure
- stronger same-region preference for carbon-aware strategies
- urgency penalties for slower cross-region fulfillment

This produced a more realistic green-fast fulfillment comparison.
The refined recommended strategy is: Carbon-first.

Important disclosure:
These results still rely on synthetic carbon and lane assumptions for comparative planning analysis.
They should not be presented as measured emissions or execution-grade transport optimization.
