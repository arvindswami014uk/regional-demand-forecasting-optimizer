DAY 15 FINAL EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=================================

Decision objective:
Build a practical decision-support workflow that forecasts regional demand, quantifies inventory risk, and recommends a deployable fulfillment strategy.

Headline findings:
- XGBoost was the best forecasting model at 11.06% WMAPE.
- Prophet was close at 11.16%, while the MA7 baseline was materially weaker at 19.25%.
- The selected model improved weighted forecast accuracy by 42.5% versus baseline.
- Inventory policy analysis estimated safety stock value at \$157,292.60 and replenishment need at \$780,850.78.
- 41 SKU-region combinations were flagged as high urgency, with average days of supply at 6.3 and zero critical combinations.

Recommendation:
- Use XGBoost as the forecasting recommendation.
- Use policy-based safety stock and reorder point logic as the operating inventory layer.
- Preserve Carbon-first as the strongest unconstrained sustainability scenario insight.
- Recommend Balanced as the final constrained strategy for deployment.

Why Balanced is the final recommendation:
Balanced provides the strongest decision under practical constraints and governance rules. It preserves the value of comparative scenario analysis without confusing unconstrained frontier performance with deployable operating policy.

Governance note:
Carbon metrics in this project are synthetic comparative proxies for planning use only. They are suitable for scenario comparison, not formal ESG disclosure.

Next steps:
- Operationalize planner overrides and KPI monitoring
- Revisit high-urgency combinations with targeted service review
- Extend the model with richer lead-time uncertainty and capacity logic
