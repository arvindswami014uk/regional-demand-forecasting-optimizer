Day 13 Board Memo — Regional Demand Forecasting + Inventory Optimization

Decision
Adopt XGBoost-based regional demand forecasting, retain policy-based inventory controls, and deploy a Carbon-first fulfillment strategy under the current network proxy.

Why now
The current process opportunity is not just better forecasting. It is better commercial control across demand, inventory, and fulfillment decisions.

What changed
- Forecast WMAPE improved from 19.25% to 11.06%
- Improvement versus strongest baseline: 42.5%
- Inventory policy now quantifies $157,292.60 in safety stock and $780,850.78 in replenishment needs
- 41 SKU-region combinations need prioritized attention

Management insight
Under the current synthetic network design, the lower-carbon option also performs well operationally because same-region routing lowers both emissions and lead time.

Governance note
Carbon results are suitable for strategy comparison, not external ESG reporting.

Executive recommendation
Proceed with Carbon-first as the default planning recommendation, but preserve Speed-first as a tactical override when service or cost thresholds are at risk.
