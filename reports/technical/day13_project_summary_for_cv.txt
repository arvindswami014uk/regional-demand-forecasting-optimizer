Regional Demand Forecasting + Inventory Optimization
Developed an end-to-end supply chain analytics capstone in Python and Google Colab that improved regional demand forecast accuracy, translated forecasts into inventory policies, and evaluated fulfillment strategy trade-offs across service, cost, and sustainability proxy metrics. XGBoost reduced WMAPE from 19.25% to 11.06%, while downstream inventory logic quantified safety stock, storage cost, and replenishment requirements. Final deliverables included executive-ready recommendations, governance documentation, and presentation materials.
